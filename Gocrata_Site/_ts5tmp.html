Gocrata History and Goals

Gocrata was concieved by Glavin Wiechert at the three day long Halifax Open Data Hackathon, as a result of issues with the preexisting data sets. To make this possible, Glavin formed a team with Liam Coleman-Aulenbach and Scott Wesley.s At the end of the three day period a prototype was produced, capible of processing the data and storing it in a queriable format

Due to other commitements, Glavin was not able to continue work on the Gocrata project.
